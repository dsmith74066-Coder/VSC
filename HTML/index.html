Biography

My name is David, and my amateur radio callsign is KJ5LVN. I was born in Arizona during the 1970s and spent my early years there. Later, I relocated to Oklahoma, where I attended Glenpool Schools in Glenpool.

Upon graduating from high school, I pursued higher education at Yavapai College, located in Prescott, Arizona. After completing my studies there, I returned to Oklahoma, and I currently reside in Sapulpa.

In April 2025, I was officially granted my callsign, KJ5LVN, marking the beginning of my journey in the amateur radio community. As a newcomer to this hobby, I have quickly developed a strong passion for ham radio. I enjoy learning new things every day, engaging with fellow operators, and making connections across the bands. I look forward to continuing my growth and involvement in this exciting and rewarding pastime.
